About Naveen Apartments

Our Story

Established in 2014, Naveen Apartments has been a beacon of modern living in Dwarka, New Delhi. Our community of 63 flats across 9 floors represents the perfect blend of contemporary architecture and comfortable living in one of Delhi's most sought-after locations.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, secure, and comfortable living environment where residents can enjoy a high quality of life with world-class amenities and professional CGHS management services.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred residential community in Dwarka, known for our exceptional service, modern facilities, and strong community bonds.

Location Advantages

  • Close to BGS International School
  • Adjacent to DDA parks for peaceful environment
  • Near Indira Gandhi International Airport
  • Well-connected to Delhi Metro
  • Shopping malls and markets in proximity
  • Excellent healthcare facilities nearby

+

The Delhi Cooperative Societies Act, 2003 provides the legal framework for the formation and functioning of cooperative housing societies in Delhi and NCR region.
